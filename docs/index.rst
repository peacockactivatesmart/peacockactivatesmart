How to Activate Peacock on a Smart TV
=====================================

Follow these easy steps:

1. Turn on your Smart TV and connect it to the internet.
2. Open the app store on your TV.
3. Download and install the Peacock app if it is not already installed.
4. Launch the Peacock app.
5. Select **Sign In**.
6. An activation code will appear on your TV screen.
7. Using another device, open a web browser.
8. Visit the official Peacock activation page.
9. Enter the activation code exactly as displayed.
10. Sign in to your Peacock account if prompted.
11. Wait while the activation is completed.
12. Your TV screen will refresh automatically, and you can begin streaming.

Common Smart TV Activation Problems
===================================

Activation Code Not Working
---------------------------

If your activation code isn't accepted:

* Verify that you entered the code correctly.
* Check whether the code has expired.
* Restart the Peacock app to generate a new code.
* Enter the new code immediately.

Unable to Sign In
-----------------

If you cannot access your account:

* Verify your email address.
* Confirm your password.
* Reset your password if necessary.
* Ensure your Peacock subscription is active.

TV Does Not Refresh
-------------------

If your Smart TV does not activate:

* Confirm your internet connection.
* Restart the Peacock app.
* Restart your Smart TV.
* Generate a new activation code and try again.

App Not Available
-----------------

If you cannot find the Peacock app:

* Verify that your TV model is supported.
* Update your TV software.
* Search the app store again.
* Install the latest version of the application.

Tips for a Successful Activation

For the best experience:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
=================================

Can I activate Peacock without an activation code?
--------------------------------------------------

Some Smart TVs may allow direct sign-in with your account credentials, but many supported models use an activation code for a faster and more secure sign-in experience.

Do I need to activate Peacock every time I use my TV?
-----------------------------------------------------

No. Activation is generally required only when signing in for the first time or after signing out of the app.

Can I use the same Peacock account on multiple Smart TVs?
---------------------------------------------------------

Yes. You can sign in to your Peacock account on multiple supported devices, subject to the streaming limits associated with your subscription.

Why did my activation code expire?
----------------------------------

Activation codes are temporary for security purposes. If your code expires, reopen the Peacock app to generate a new one.

What should I do if activation still fails?
-------------------------------------------

Restart your Smart TV, verify your internet connection, generate a new activation code, and ensure you're signing in with the correct Peacock account. If the issue continues, consult the official Peacock Help Center for additional support.
